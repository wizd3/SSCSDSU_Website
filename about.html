~ About Us ~

The Saudi Students Club at San Diego State University was founded in Fall 2009 in order to serve the Saudi students in all fields.

The Saudi Students Club seeks to strengthen the bonds of brotherhood, and coheres the Saudi students during their scholarship period by hosting differnt types of events throughout the year.

The Saudi Students Club represents Saudi Arabia and presents its culture and history to fellow classmates and faculty from all other countries.

Beyond all of that, The Saudi Students Club informs the Saudi students about SDSU's activities and programs whether they are academic, cultural, social, or athletic.
